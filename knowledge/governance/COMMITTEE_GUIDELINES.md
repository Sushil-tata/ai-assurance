## Metadata
- Document Owner: AI Assurance Product and Governance Team
- Version: 1.0
- Effective Date: July 2026
- Review Frequency: Quarterly
- Status: Draft for Training Demonstration

## Purpose
Define standard committee-pack structure and decision expectations for model governance actions.

## Scope
Applies to Amber and Red escalations and any action requiring human approval.

## Committee Pack Structure
- Executive conclusion.
- Model status.
- Findings.
- Root causes.
- Evidence summary.
- Recommendation.
- Decision requested.
- Action owner.
- Due date.

## Minimum Quality Rules
- Executive conclusion must state severity and decision ask in plain language.
- Findings must be evidence-backed and policy-referenced.
- Recommendation must include alternatives considered.
- Decision log must include rationale and any modifications.

## Exclusions From Executive Reporting
- Raw row-level extracts.
- Sensitive or confidential identifiers.
- Unvalidated intermediate calculations.
- Internal debate notes not tied to final evidence-backed conclusion.
