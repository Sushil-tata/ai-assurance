# Human Approval Policy

## Metadata
- Document Owner: AI Assurance Product and Governance Team
- Version: 1.0
- Effective Date: July 2026
- Review Frequency: Quarterly
- Status: Draft for Training Demonstration

## Purpose
Define mandatory human decision points for material model governance actions.

## Scope
Applies to recommendations generated by AI Assurance monitoring, investigation, and governance agents.

## Outputs Requiring Human Approval
- Recalibration proposals.
- Temporary use restriction proposals.
- Redevelopment and retirement recommendations.
- Material applicability and implementation exceptions.
- Committee-level escalation outcomes.

## Allowed Human Decisions
- Approve.
- Approve with modification.
- Request further analysis.
- Reject.
- Defer.

## Override Requirement
- Any decision that modifies the recommended action must include rationale.
- Rationale should reference evidence IDs, policy basis, and expected impact.

## Hard Control Statement
AI Assurance cannot independently deploy, recalibrate, restrict, or retire a model.

## Decision Record Minimum Fields
- Decision type.
- Decision maker and role.
- Timestamp.
- Rationale.
- Related evidence IDs.
- Follow-up action owner and due date.
