## Practical Themes
- Maintain a complete model inventory with ownership and status.
- Define governance roles and clear accountability.
- Require independent validation and periodic re-validation.
- Run ongoing monitoring for stability, discrimination, calibration, and applicability.
- Enforce human oversight for material model decisions.
- Maintain strong data quality controls and lineage.
- Support explainability appropriate to use case and risk.
- Manage lifecycle controls for change, recalibration, redevelopment, and retirement.
