## Important Caveat
This is a practical summary for training demonstration and is not legal or regulatory advice.

## FEAT Themes
### Fairness
- Monitor outcomes across relevant segments.
- Investigate and explain material disparities.

### Ethics
- Ensure model use aligns with intended business purpose and customer treatment principles.

### Accountability
- Assign named owners and clear decision authority.
- Require human approval for material actions.

### Transparency
- Keep understandable documentation of model purpose, limits, and governance decisions.

## Practical Relevance to Financial AI
- Supports evidence-backed investigation discipline.
- Supports explainable committee decisions.
- Supports controlled remediation and accountability traceability.
