## Important Caveat

## Practical Themes
- Explainability: provide understandable rationale for model behavior and decisions.
- Validation: perform robust independent validation and ongoing challenge.
- Performance: monitor discrimination, calibration, and population stability.
- Data governance: control data quality, lineage, and representativeness.
- Scope control: ensure model is used only within validated scope and assumptions.
